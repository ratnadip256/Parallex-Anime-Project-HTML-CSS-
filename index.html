WELCOME TO JUJUTSU KAISEN

SATORU GOJO

• Satoru Gojo is one of the main protagonists of the popular manga and anime series Jujutsu Kaisen. He is widely recognized as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer in the world, a title he earned by inheriting both the Limitless and the Six Eyes techniques, a feat not achieved in over 400 years. Gojo is a proud member of the Gojo Clan and serves as a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High, where he mentors and protects his students with unparalleled skill and dedication.

• Despite his immense power, Gojo is known for his carefree and playful personality. He often covers his eyes with a black blindfold, which he can see through thanks to his supernatural abilities. His bright-blue eyes, known as the Six Eyes, grant him extraordinary powers, including heightened perception and the ability to manipulate cursed energy. Gojo's charisma and unwavering commitment to his students make him a beloved character among fans of the series.
